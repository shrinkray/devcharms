DevCharms is my space for **practical engineering writing**: what worked, what failed, and what I would do differently next time. It is not a theme demo and not a content mill—just notes I would want to read while debugging something at midnight.

## Colophon

The site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build/), styled with [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/), and uses a little React for the search experience. Posts are plain Markdown; the feed lives at [`/rss.xml`](/rss.xml).

If something here saves you time, that is enough. Feedback and corrections are welcome via the links in the footer.
