**DevCharms** takes its name from an old image: a **bracelet of small charms**, each one marking something you care about. On this site, **tags** are starting to work the same way—each topic can earn its own **charm**: a tiny, recognizable emblem you collect as you read.

## What we are building

The idea is simple to say and fun to iterate on:

- One **charm** per **tag** (or per story theme), so scanning tags feels like scanning a curated set of tokens—not a flat list of strings.
- Charms are imagined as **small 3D objects**—like metal or glass blocks on a bracelet—so they read clearly at icon size and still feel like _things_, not just flat glyphs.
- Some may use **two-letter marks** (like **PY**), others a **stylized motif** inspired by a language or tool—whatever reads fastest and stays **original** to this site.

Nothing here is final. This page is a **living stub**: as charms are designed and wired into tag pages and cards, this section will collect **notes, previews, and the story behind the set**.
